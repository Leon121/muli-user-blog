Steps for installation on Windows:

(You must have Python installed on your system)

1 - Download the .zip folder
2 - Extract files to a folder on your Desktop
3 - Open cmp and navigate to the folder
4 - Once you are in the folder directory run the comand: dev_appserver.py . (include the period after .py)
5 - A message will appear for you to locate your app in the address: http://localhost:8080
6 - Open your browser and enter http://localhost:8080 in the search bar and click on signup
7 - Enter you username and preferred password (email is optional)
7 - Start bloging
